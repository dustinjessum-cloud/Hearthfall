Peace — first raid in 3:30
Click a building below, then click the map to place it. Right-click or Esc cancels. Arrow keys/WASD to pan, wheel to zoom. Click a villager, then right-click a Farm/Lumber Camp/Quarry to put them to work there (or an empty tile to send them off, unassigning them). Click a Barracks to convert a villager into an Archer (ranged, fragile) or Swordsman (tough melee — auto-charges nearby raiders). Select the Town Hall, Barracks or Mason and right-click the map to set a RALLY POINT — new units march there automatically (right-click the building to clear it). P pauses the game. Damaged walls & towers are fixed by REPAIRMEN (trained at the Mason, from a villager): right-click damage with one selected; they only work on your orders. Buildings take time to construct, and any building can be salvaged for 15% of its cost (3 gold fee). NEW: placing a building only reserves the site — a villager must physically walk over before construction actually starts (the game auto-sends an idle one; the faint "foundation" look means nobody's arrived yet). Walls (and Towers) now block your OWN units' movement too, just like they block raiders — build a GATE into a wall line to let your people through while still stopping the enemy. Water is impassable to everyone, no exceptions. Lumber camps & quarries employ up to 3 villagers each. NEW: a few remote deposits of WILDSTONE dot the map (pinkish crystal) — once your Town Hall reaches level 3, build a REFINERY directly on one to start extracting it (same idea as a lumber camp, just built right on the vein). Not required for anything basic, but it funds permanent EVOLUTIONS at the Barracks — Veteran Training and Masterwork Bows upgrade every Swordsman/Archer you have, forever, once researched. Click the Town Hall to upgrade it: more HP, storage, population, faster settlers — and at level 3 it shoots back. NEW: upgrades (Town Hall and storage buildings alike) now take real time to complete once started, not just resources — the cost is paid up front, then a countdown runs before the bonus actually lands. Raids no longer pause your economy — workers keep toiling unless YOU act: Recall Workers garrisons every worker INSIDE the Town Hall (each adds defense damage, capped, at any TC level); press it again and they walk back to the exact jobs they left. Single villagers can also be garrisoned by right-clicking the TC. NEW: Farms grow wheat → staffed Mills grind flour → staffed Bakeries bake food (1.5x). Houses pay gold taxes; Wells & Taverns raise happiness. Soldiers eat double rations and buildings sip wood upkeep — keep the woodpile stocked or things decay. Bandit camps on the frontier send PILLAGERS (orange) who ignore your walls' purpose — they hunt farms, camps, mills and markets directly; from wave 2 raids bring them too. Raze camps with soldiers for loot. Recruit a Minotaur hero at the Town Hall (gold) — nearby soldiers fight 25% harder, but HE fights only on your command: J hurls a javelin toward the mouse, K slashes adjacent foes. He levels up from kills near him and keeps his level through revival.
